1️⃣ Warm-Up (~10 minutes)  
- 30s jumping jacks  
- 30s alternating reverse lunges  
- 30s arm circles (forward/backward)  
- 30s bodyweight good mornings  
- 30s high knees in place  
Repeat 2 rounds  

2️⃣ TABATA Core (~12 minutes)  

TABATA Core – Version A (8 rounds, 20s work / 10s rest)  
- Dead bug – https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4XLEnwUr1d8  
- Modified sit-up – https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_HDZODOx7Zw  
- Glute bridge – https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hwrC-ngRsoY  
- Forearm plank hold – https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4ITM-VMO8eI  

3️⃣ EMOM Strength (10 minutes)  

10-minute EMOM (Every Minute On the Minute):  
- Minute 1: Push-ups – https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_l3ySVKYVJ8  
- Minute 2: Air squats – https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rMvwVtlqjTE  
Repeat for a total of 10 minutes (5 rounds each)  

4️⃣ High-Intensity Finisher (~15 minutes)  

For Time Version
