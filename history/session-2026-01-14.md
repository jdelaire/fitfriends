1️⃣ Warm-Up (~10 minutes)  
- 30s easy jump rope (or simulated rope)  
- 30s arm circles + arm hugs  
- 30s hamstring sweeps (straight-leg kicks)  
- 30s alternating reverse lunges  
- 30s plank to down-dog  
Repeat 2 rounds
