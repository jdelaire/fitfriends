1️⃣ Warm-Up (~10 minutes)  
- 30s light jog in place  
- 30s jumping jacks  
- 30s alternating knee hugs (standing)  
- 30s bodyweight good mornings  
- 30s air squats  
Repeat 2 rounds  

2️⃣ TABATA Core (~12 minutes)  

TABATA Core (8 rounds, 20s work / 10s rest)  
- Hollow body hold – https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EsnM8eBtazU  
- Side plank – https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OKoN7ZYJhX4  
- Mountain climbers – https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ruQ4ZwncXBg  
- V-ups – https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iP2fjvG0g3w  

3️⃣ EMOM Strength (10 minutes)  

10-minute EMOM (Every Minute On the Minute):  
- Minute 1: 12 Jump squats – https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=87ynaOgFplw  
- Minute 2: 12 Push-Ups – https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_l3ySVKYVJ8 
Repeat for a total of 10 minutes (5 rounds each)  

4️⃣ High-Intensity Finisher

12-minute AMRAP: complete as many rounds as possible of:  
- 6 Burpees – https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TU8QYVW0gDU  
- 12 Jump squats – https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=87ynaOgFplw  
- 16 Reverse lunges (8/side) – https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ry-wqegeKlE
