1️⃣ Warm-Up (~10 minutes)  
- 30s easy jump rope (or simulated rope)  
- 30s butt kicks  
- 30s lateral shuffles (touch a line)  
- 30s alternating reverse lunges  
- 30s squat-to-stand  
Repeat 2 rounds

3️⃣ EMOM Strength (10 minutes)  

10-minute EMOM (Every Minute On the Minute):
